Help & Care

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I wash the reusable pads?

Rinse in cold water after use, then machine wash in a laundry pouch. Air‑dry for best results.

Are the wipes safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. They are alcohol‑free and pH‑balanced. Always discontinue use if irritation occurs.

What sizes are available?

Day, Night, and Super. See the size guide on the Features page.

What makes PureGlow eco‑friendly?

Reusable materials, minimal plastics, and recyclable packaging reduce waste over time.
